The VuMark sample shows how to detect and track VuMarks.

Key Functionality

  • Simultaneous detection and tracking of multiple VuMarks
  • Load and activate a VuMark target
  • Activate Extended Tracking
  • Render an outline when a VuMark is detected

Target

  • Included VuMarks

Instructions

  • Point device at VuMark
  • Single tap to focus
  • Double tap to access options menu
  • Tap window showing VuMark ID to dismiss
